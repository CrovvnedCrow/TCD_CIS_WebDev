Quick Background

ZZZ is hoyos 4th major gacha release based in a futuristic tech focused city. These big bubbles called hollows are appearing and growing bigger, filling with monsters called ethereals and people are sent out to explore them to get rid of them. Theres also a gruop trying to bring the 'creator' back in an attempt to reset the world. You play as a proxy, someone who guides agents through hollows safely, that helps take down this group and rid the world of the hollows while finding what happened to your teacher.

Color Scheme

Our electric color palette creates a modern, high-tech aesthetic:

Magenta (#ff00ff)

Primary accent color used for headings, borders, and interactive elements. Creates energy and draws attention.

Cyan (#00d4ff)

Secondary accent color used for text highlights and hover states. Provides contrast and visual interest.

Cobalt (#0f1a3d)

Primary background color. Deep, dark blue creates a professional tech aesthetic.

Navy (#0a0e27)

Darkest background color. Used for the main page background and code examples.
